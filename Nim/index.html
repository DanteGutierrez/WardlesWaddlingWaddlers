How to play Nim:

You and an opponent take turns removing the harpoons from the field.

You may take any number of harpoons from any row, but you may only take from a single row per turn

The person to take the last harpoon on the field is the loser.

Click the 'Switch Turns' button in the middle of the screen above the board to hand your turn off to the other player

Putting your name in the boxes in either the upper right or left of the screen will set your name for the round

This button will restart the game at the current difficulty

These buttons will determine the difficulty

Clicking the button below these groups of harpoons will lock you into taking from that stack, click the button multiple times to take multiple harpoons
